AirGuard™ Safety Collar

The AirGuard™ uses a 6-axis IMU (inertial measurement unit) — a combination of accelerometer and gyroscope — sampling motion 800 times per second. Our proprietary FallSense™ AI algorithm runs on the device's embedded processor and is trained to distinguish a genuine fall from normal activities like sitting down quickly, bending over, or bumping into furniture. The algorithm calibrates to the wearer's individual gait and movement patterns over the first few days of use.
After deployment: (1) the airbag cushion remains inflated for several minutes to protect during the recovery period, (2) the MEND Guardian App sends an automatic alert with GPS location to your designated emergency contacts within ~2 seconds, and (3) the device logs the fall event for review. A replacement CO₂ canister ships automatically if you have our Guardian subscription. The fabric shell is machine washable — only the CO₂ canister needs replacement after each deployment.
In our clinical trials, the AirGuard™ had a false trigger rate of under 0.5% — meaning fewer than 1 in 200 wearable-days resulted in an unintended deployment. For context, the device handles high-motion activities like walking on uneven terrain, light exercise, and household tasks without triggering. If a false trigger does occur, replacement CO₂ canisters are available at cost.
The AirGuard™ is currently in pre-order. Estimated ship date is Q3 2026, pending FDA 510(k) clearance. Pre-order customers will be notified first and charged only at time of shipment. You can cancel your pre-order at any time before shipment with a full refund.

Sizing & Fit

MEND gowns and scrubs are sized XS–3XL. Our sizing runs true to standard US sizing. For gowns, we recommend ordering your usual top size for the best fit. For compression socks, measure the circumference of your calf at its widest point and refer to our size chart: S/M (12–16"), L/XL (16–20"), XL/XXL (20"+).

Care Instructions

Machine wash cold, gentle cycle, with like colors. Tumble dry low or lay flat to dry. Do not bleach. Do not iron directly on embroidered areas. Our cotton-merino blend is designed to maintain softness and shape through repeated washing — tested to 100+ wash cycles with no significant pilling or shrinkage.
The fabric shell of the AirGuard™ is machine washable. Remove the sensor unit (detaches with a magnetic snap) and the CO₂ canister before washing. The shell can be washed on a cold gentle cycle and air dried. Never machine wash or submerge the electronic sensor module.
