Hospital gowns shouldn't feel like hospital gowns. Comfort Wrap is a knee-length, form-fitting robe made from a lightweight cotton/merino wool blend — soft against the skin, breathable, and durable enough for repeated hot-water washing. An attached hood adds warmth and a loungewear feel, while a full-length zipper (front or back) replaces the traditional gap-prone tie closure, so there's no exposure and no fumbling with ties. A discreet chest pocket holds a telemetry monitor, and shoulder-seam zips give clinicians quick IV access without removing the gown.
