Why It's Different

Other wearables detect falls after the fact. AirGuard™ prevents the injury.

📡

Medical-Grade Sensor Fusion

A 6-axis IMU samples motion 800 times per second. Our FallSense™ AI distinguishes between tripping, stumbling, and an actual fall within 12ms.

💨

Cold-Gas Inflation — No Burns

Unlike automotive airbags, AirGuard™ uses medical-grade CO₂ — cold, clean, and rechargeable. Safe for skin contact and suitable for daily wear.

🛡

Head & Neck Protection Zone

The cushion deploys to cover the base of skull, the cervical spine, and the temples — the highest-risk impact zones in a backward or sideways fall.

📱

Guardian App + Family Alerts

When deployment occurs, the MEND Guardian App automatically notifies your emergency contacts with GPS location within 2 seconds of the event.
