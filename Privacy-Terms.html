5. AirGuard™ Health & Location Data

The MEND AirGuard™ safety collar and associated MEND Guardian App collect sensitive data including:

  • Motion data: accelerometer and gyroscope readings used for fall detection. This data is processed on-device and not stored on our servers except as anonymized aggregate data for model improvement.
  • Location data: GPS coordinates sent to emergency contacts upon fall detection. Location is transmitted only at the moment of deployment — we do not track your location continuously.
  • Fall event logs: timestamped records of deployments stored securely in your account and accessible only by you and designated emergency contacts.

We do not sell AirGuard™ health or location data to any third party. De-identified motion data may be used to improve the FallSense™ detection algorithm. You may request deletion of all AirGuard™ data at any time by contacting us.

6. Data Retention

We retain your order information for 7 years as required for tax and legal compliance. Account information is retained until you request deletion. Email marketing preferences are retained until you unsubscribe. Contact form messages are retained for 2 years.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@mendmedicalwear.com. We respond to all requests within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website and products are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

9. Security

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure — we cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your data.
